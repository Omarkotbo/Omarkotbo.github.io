Geometry and Space: Copernican revolution in astronomy

By/ Omar Mohamed Hussien Kotb


Abstract

Most people are unaware that a century and a half ago, a revolution in geometry occurred that was as scientifically profound as the Copernican revolution in astronomy and as philosophically significant as the Darwinian theory of evolution. Greenberg 1974. It is a widespread belief that geometry, with all of its truths, is universally applicable to all men, all times, and all peoples, and not just all historically factual ones, but all conceivable ones.
